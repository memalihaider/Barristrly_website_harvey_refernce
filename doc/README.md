# Overview

Barristrly is an AI-powered legal services marketplace that connects clients with verified lawyers through an anonymous, intelligent, and secure platform.

Unlike traditional legal marketplaces where users manually search for lawyers, Barristrly uses Artificial Intelligence to understand a client's legal issue, screen for conflicts of interest, recommend the best lawyer, facilitate secure meetings, and manage the complete business workflow from intake through qualified lead conversion.

The platform consists of:

- Web Application
- Android Application
- iOS Application
- AI Chatbot
- AI Voice Agent
- Video Meeting Platform
- CRM
- POS
- Accounting
- Subscription Management
- Advertisement Platform
- AI Administration Tools
